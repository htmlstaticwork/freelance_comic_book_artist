Quick FAQ

The most common questions, answered upfront.

What files do you deliver?

Layered working files plus flattened exports sized for print and web.

Do you work from scripts or outlines?

Both. Full scripts are ideal, but strong outlines can work with a quick planning call.

How many revision rounds are included?

Typically 2 rounds on thumbnails/pencils and 1 light pass on inks (project-dependent).

What’s the usual turnaround time?

It depends on complexity and schedule, but I’ll confirm a timeline before we begin.
